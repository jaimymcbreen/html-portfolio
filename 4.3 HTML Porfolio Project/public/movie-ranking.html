Top 3 Movies

The top 3 films according to me.

Empire Strikes Back

This is the pinnacle of Star Wars. The stage has already been set and the characters develop more than in the first movie. Plus, Boba Fett.

Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade

This is always a toss-up between Raiders and Last Crusade. I think this one wins by a hair. It has Sean Connery and that cool Templar guy.

Scott Pilgrim Vs the World

Scott Pilgrim is just a ton of fun. Comic books, music and video games all rolled into one kickass movie.
